skychat*

secure mesh chat

FEATURES

  • Offline Communication

    Works without internet using Bluetooth mesh networking—devices relay through nearby peers for resilient, local‑first delivery.

  • End‑to‑End Encryption

    Modern cryptography (Curve25519 + AES‑GCM) ensures only intended recipients can read your messages—operators and relays cannot.

  • Extended Range

    Peer‑to‑peer hops extend coverage across venues and trails so your team stays connected even without towers or Wi‑Fi.

  • Favorites System

    Trusted contacts buffer messages while you’re offline and deliver automatically once you reconnect to the mesh.

  • Mentions

    @nickname pings the right person in a busy room, cutting through noise without blasting notifications to everyone.

  • Rooms

    Create #channels to organize discussions by topic with scoped keys so only the right people can read.

  • Password Rooms

    Add a shared passphrase on top of encryption for ad‑hoc privacy—great for pop‑up groups and temporary teams.

PRIVACY

  • No Tracking

    No accounts, numbers, or telemetry—there’s nothing central to sell, subpoena, or leak.

  • Ephemeral Identity

    Rotating IDs make long‑term correlation difficult, limiting what observers can infer about you.

  • Safekeeping

    A local encrypted vault protects backups and keys so recovery never compromises your privacy.
